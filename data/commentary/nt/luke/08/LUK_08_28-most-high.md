title: Most High

---

The demons recognize Jesus by name and refer to him as "Son of the Most High God," using a title that reflects the Old Testament's language about God as the "Most High" (ὕψιστος / *hypsistos*)[^mosthigh] and thus acknowledges Jesus' unique authority over the spiritual realm.


[^mosthigh]: *Cf.* [Deuteronomy 32:8](ref:DEU 32:8); [Psalm 81(82):6](ref:PSA 81:6); [Daniel 4:17](ref:DAN 4:17)
