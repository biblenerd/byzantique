title: Tassel
tags: translation
---

She "touched the fringe of his cloak." "Fringe" here is κράσπεδον (*kraspedon*), which may refer to the "tassel (צִיצִת) which an Israelite was obligated to wear on the four corners of his outer garment."[^bdag]


[^bdag]: BDAG, 564; *cf.* also [Numbers 15:37–39](ref:NUM 15:37-39).
