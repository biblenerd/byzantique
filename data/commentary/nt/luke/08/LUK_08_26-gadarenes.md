anchor: LUK 8:26
title: Gadarenes
tags: geography, translation
---

Jesus and his disciples "sailed to the region of the Gadarenes, which is across the lake from Galilee." This is also known as "Gerasenes" or "Gergesenes," and refers to the city of Gerasa (which is modern Jerash, Jordan), where he encountered "a man from the city who had been possessed by demons for a long time" ([v. 27](ref:LUK 8:27)). This was part of ancient Gilead.
