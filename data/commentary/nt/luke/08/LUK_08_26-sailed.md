[GRCBYZ (ANT)](/about/texts-and-translations/#grcbyz) here has the third-person *singular* verb κατέπλευσεν (*katepleusen*, "he sailed down"), whereas almost all other compared editions have the third-person *plural* form of this verb (κατέπλευσαν / *katepleusan*). Even so, third-person singular verbs can take plural subjects, and [v. 22](ref:LUK 8:22) indicates his disciples were with him in the boat.
