title: Twelve years
tags: symbolism
---

The healing of the "suffered from a flow of blood for twelve years" ([v. 43](ref:LUK 8:43)) is interjected in the middle of the account of Jesus healing Jairus' daughter.

The woman in the crowd has been bleeding for the entire lifespan of Jairus' daughter: 12 years ([v. 42](ref:LUK 8:42)). The number itself of course has additional symbolism, and everyone in these accounts has been waiting for Jesus (see also [v. 40](ref:LUK 8:40)).
