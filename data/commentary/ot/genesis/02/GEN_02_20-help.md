St. Augustine and other Fathers believed the primary way in which Eve was a help to Adam was her ability to bear children.[^aug] However, others believed that Adam and Eve lived in virginity prior to the Fall so look to other attributes in which she was his help.


[^aug]: *Cf.* St. Augustine, *On the Literal Interpretation of Genesis* 9.5.9.
