The Hebrew contains an etymological pun on "man" (אָדָם / *adam*) and "dust" / "ground" (אֲדָמָה / *adamah*). Robert Alter's translation which seeks to maintain this wordplay in English is clever: "… then the LORD God fashioned the human, humus from the soil…"[^alter]


[^alter]: Robert Alter, ed., *The Hebrew Bible: A Translation with Commentary*, 3 volumes (New York; London: W. W. Norton & Company, 2019), Ge 2:7.
