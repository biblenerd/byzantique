title: Adam slept
tags: 
---

The Fathers discuss God's mercy in causing Adam to sleep so as to not feel any pain during this process. Contemporary commentators have speculated that Adam being asleep emphasizes God's creative role in the creation of woman&mdash;Adam contributed part of himself but personally was not any kind of co-creator of woman.

The Fathers also commented on the process of God removing Adam's rib, and some fathers believe God caused him to dream for the first time during this process, whereas others focus on the wound on Jesus' side compared to the "wound" of removing Adam’s rib.
