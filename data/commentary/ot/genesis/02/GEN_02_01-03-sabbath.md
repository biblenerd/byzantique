---
anchor: GEN 2:1-3
title: Sabbath
---

On the sixth day, God indicated that his work was finished and He instituted and rested on the Sabbath. Likewise, Jesus indicated that "It is finished" on the Cross ([John 19:30](ref:JHN 19:30)), and then proceeded to rest on the Sabbath (creation –> new creation). In both instances, this is not to take a break because God is tired, but rather, God is taking His seat as King and Lord. Where man failed to keep this Sabbath-rest, Jesus fulfilled it on man's behalf, resting in the tomb on Great and Holy Saturday, destroying sin and death, and rising on the first day of the week. He is thus man's Sabbath-rest, inviting all to find rest in Himself ([Mt 11:28–30](ref:MAT 11:28-30)).
