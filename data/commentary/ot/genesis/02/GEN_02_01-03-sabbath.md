anchor: GEN 2:1-3
title: Sabbath
---

On the sixth day, God indicated that his work was finished and He instituted and rested on the Sabbath. Likewise, Jesus indicated that "It is finished" on the Cross ([John 19:30](ref:JHN 19:30)), and then proceeded to rest on the Sabbath (creation –> new creation). In both instances, this is not to take a break because God is tired, but rather, God is taking His seat as King and Lord.

>Man failed to keep this Sabbath-rest. But Jesus fulfilled it for man by resting in the tomb on Great and Holy Saturday, after He said on the cross, “It is finished” ([Jn 19:30](ref:JHN 19:30)). For He destroyed sin and death, and rose again on the first day of the week. Through His saving work on man's behalf, He is man’s Sabbath-rest, and He now invites all to find rest in Himself ([Mt 11:28–30](ref:MAT 11:28-30)).[^osb]


[^osb]: OSB, 5.
