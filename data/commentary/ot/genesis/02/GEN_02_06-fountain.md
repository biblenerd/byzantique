The Hebrew word for "fountain" (or "stream") is challenging to translate (Hebrew: אֵד / *ēd*; Greek: πηγή / *pēgē*). It may refer to "the subterranean stream of fresh water, groundwater" or "the celestial stream."[^halot] A similar word appears in early Sumerian and Babylonian mythological accounts in reference to an underground primordial river.


[^halot]: HALOT, 11.
