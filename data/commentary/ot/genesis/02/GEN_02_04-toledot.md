תּוֹלֵדוֹת (*toledot*) / γενέσεως (*geneseōs*) marker on the cosmos, represented as a merism[^merism] of heaven and earth. Some do not consider this to be a true section marker. See note on [Toledot formula](note:GEN_00-toledot).

[^merism]: A merism is a pair of opposites used to denote the totality of the whole, here the ordered cosmos.
