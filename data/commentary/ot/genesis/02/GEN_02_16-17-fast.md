title: The first commandment is to fast
tags: 
---

ANE deities delivered their laws or law codes to their appointed kings, and here God commanded Adam concerning the tree of the knowledge of good and evil.

>Let us fast, my children, do not listen to those who say that fasting is nothing, and that this is something from monks. This is not from monks, my children, forgive me, God says this. The first commandment of God is fasting, and our Christ fasted.[^tsalikis]

The Fathers emphasize that the tree of knowledge of good and evil wasn't bad or evil. Humans would have eventually been invited to eat from it, but they weren’t ready yet.

Notice the commandment is here given solely to Adam, before Eve is created.[^xref]
