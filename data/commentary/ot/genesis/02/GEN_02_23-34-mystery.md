---
anchor: GEN 2:23-24
title: Profound mystery
tags: typology
---

St. Paul alludes to Adam's song/poem and calls this union as one flesh a "profound mystery," but relates this relationship between husband and wife to that between Christ and His Church, respectively ([Ephesians 5:30–32](ref:EPH 5:30-32)). Also, just as a husband must leave his parents and "cleave" to his wife, Jesus (in a sense) left the Father (becoming human) to find and redeem His Bride, the Church. The Fathers frequently comment on marriage as an example of the relationship between Jesus and the Church.
