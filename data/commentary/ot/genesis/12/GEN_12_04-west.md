title: Westward journey
tags: symbolism
---

>In Genesis 2–11 the Yahwist depicts the movement of primeval humanity in an easterly direction from the garden ([Gen 3:24](ref:GEN 3:24); [4:16](ref:GEN 4:16); [11:2](ref:GEN 11:2)). This movement is reversed in Gen 12:4 when Abraham begins his journey westward from Haran to Canaan.[^aybd]


[^aybd]: Howard N. Wallace, "Eden, Garden of (Place)," in *The Anchor Yale Bible Dictionary*, vol. 2, ed. David Noel Freedman (New York: Doubleday, 1992), 283.
