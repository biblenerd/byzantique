*Possible* תּוֹלֵדוֹת (*toledot*) / γενέσεως (*geneseōs*) marker, which specifically emphasizes Esau as the father of the Edomites. See note on [Toledot formula](note:GEN_00-toledot).

This instance is typically considered part of the *toledot* "section" that began at [36:1](ref:GEN 36:1).
