Some unspecified period of time has passed&mdash;at least enough for Cain and Abel to grow older. The Hebrew word for "offering" or "gift" (מִנְחָה / *minḥah*) may imply a grain offering (*cf.* [Leviticus 2](ref:LEV 2:1)), but does not preclude animal sacrifice.

I think θυσία (*thysia*) would here better be translated as "offering" (it refers specifically to "that which is offered as a sacrifice").[^bdag]


[^bdag]: BDAG, 462.
