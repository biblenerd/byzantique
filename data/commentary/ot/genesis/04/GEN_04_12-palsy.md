title: Palsy?
tags: 
---

St. John Chrysostom taught that Cain had a form of palsy that caused him to tremble, perhaps based on στένων καὶ τρέμων (*stenōn kai tremōn*, "groaning and trembling"). He also saw this suffering as a benefit to him from God (a medicine for his soul)&mdash;and as a warning to others.[^chrysostom] God could have killed him (capital punishment would have been appropriate under later Israelite law), but shows mercy.


[^chrysostom]: St. John Chrysostom, *Discourses against Judaizing Christians* 8.2.9–10.
