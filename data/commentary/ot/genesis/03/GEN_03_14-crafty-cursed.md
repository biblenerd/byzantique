title: Most crafty, most cursed

There is a contrast between vv. [1](ref:GEN 3:1) and 14 describing the serpent, on which Rashi commented, "Corresponding with his subtleness and his greatness was his downfall; 'more subtle than all' &mdash; 'more cursed than all'":[^rashi]

- [3:1](ref:GEN 3:1): "Now the serpent was *the most crafty of all* the brutes on the earth…."
- [3:14](ref:GEN 3:14): "Because thou hast done this thou art *cursed above all* cattle and all the brutes of the earth…."


[^rashi]: Rashi, *Commentary on Genesis*, trans. M. Rosenbaum and A. M. Silbermann (London: Shapiro, Vallentine, 1929–34), 3:1:2 (accessed via [Sefaria](https://www.sefaria.org/Rashi_on_Genesis.3.1.2)).
