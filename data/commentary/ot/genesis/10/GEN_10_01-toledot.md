תּוֹלֵדוֹת (*toledot*) / γενέσεως (*geneseōs*) marker for Noah's sons: Shem, Ham, and Japheth. See note on [Toledot formula](note:GEN_00-toledot).

It also occurs at [10:32](ref:GEN 10:32) but this is usually considered part of *this* "section."
